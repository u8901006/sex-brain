# Sexuality / Sex Counseling / Sexual Neuroscience Journals (Q1–Q3)

**Last updated:** 2026-04-14  
**Purpose:** A practical English reference list of journals relevant to **sexology**, **sex counseling / psychosexual therapy**, and **sexual neuroscience**, with topic fit, recent quartile status, common keywords, and ready-to-use **PubMed search templates**.

## Notes

- **Quartile** below refers to the **best quartile** reported by recent SCImago/Scopus-derived sources available on the web (typically reflecting 2024 data displayed in 2025–2026).
- The field of **sex counseling / psychosexual therapy** has relatively **few dedicated journals** compared with broader psychiatry, psychology, or neuroscience. To keep this list useful, I included:
  1. core sexuality journals,
  2. psychosexual / counseling-adjacent journals,
  3. sexual neuroscience / neuroendocrine journals,
  4. selected Q3 journals that are still practically useful for literature surveillance.
- PubMed templates are designed to be **editable starting points** rather than rigid formulas.
- Some social-science sexuality journals may have **partial / selective PubMed coverage**; they are still included when they are influential in the field.

## 1) Core Sexology / Sexual Medicine Journals

### 1. The Journal of Sexual Medicine
- **Positioning:** Flagship clinical and translational sexual medicine journal; male/female/couple sexual function and dysfunction, diagnostics, interventions, outcomes.
- **Best Quartile:** **Q1**
- **Common keywords:** sexual dysfunction, erectile dysfunction, premature ejaculation, female sexual dysfunction, hypoactive sexual desire disorder, orgasm disorder, dyspareunia, genitourinary syndrome of menopause, sexual pain, pelvic floor, treatment outcomes
- **PubMed template:**
```text
("The Journal of Sexual Medicine"[Journal]) AND
(
  "sexual dysfunction" OR "erectile dysfunction" OR "premature ejaculation" OR
  "female sexual dysfunction" OR "hypoactive sexual desire disorder" OR
  dyspareunia OR orgasm* OR libido OR arousal
)
```

## 6) Practical Use Tips

- For **clinical sexual dysfunction**, start with:  
  **The Journal of Sexual Medicine**, **Sexual Medicine Reviews**, **Sexual Medicine**, **Journal of Sex & Marital Therapy**
- For **sex counseling / psychosexual therapy**, start with:  
  **Journal of Sex & Marital Therapy**, **Sexual and Relationship Therapy**, **Archives of Sexual Behavior**, **Psychology & Sexuality**
- For **sexual neuroscience / hormones**, start with:  
  **Psychoneuroendocrinology**, **Hormones and Behavior**, plus sexuality-specific papers in **The Journal of Sexual Medicine**
- For **sexuality education / social context / policy**, start with:  
  **The Journal of Sex Research**, **Sexuality Research and Social Policy**, **Sexualities**, **Sex Education**, **Sexuality & Culture**
- For **rehabilitation / disability / chronic illness and sexuality**, start with:  
  **Sexuality and Disability** and **Sexual Health**
