# Sexuality / Sex Counseling / Sexual Neuroscience Journals

**Last updated:** 2026-04-14  
**Purpose:** A practical English reference list of journals relevant to **sexology**, **sex counseling / psychosexual therapy**, and **sexual neuroscience**, with topic fit, recent quartile status, common keywords, and ready-to-use **PubMed search templates**.

## Notes

- **Quartile** below refers to the **best quartile** reported by recent SCImago/Scopus-derived sources available on the web (typically reflecting 2024 data displayed in 2025–2026).
- The field of **sex counseling / psychosexual therapy** has relatively **few dedicated high-quartile journals**. To make this list useful, I included:
  1. core sexuality journals,
  2. psychosexual / counseling-adjacent journals,
  3. sexual neuroscience / neuroendocrine journals that frequently publish sexuality-related work.
- PubMed templates are designed to be **editable starting points** rather than rigid formulas.

## 1) Core Sexology / Sexual Medicine Journals

### 1. The Journal of Sexual Medicine
- **Positioning:** Flagship clinical and translational sexual medicine journal; male/female/couple sexual function and dysfunction, diagnostics, interventions, outcomes.
- **Best Quartile:** **Q1**
- **Common keywords:** sexual dysfunction, erectile dysfunction, premature ejaculation, female sexual dysfunction, hypoactive sexual desire disorder, orgasm disorder, dyspareunia, genitourinary syndrome of menopause, sexual pain, pelvic floor, treatment outcomes
- **PubMed template:**
```text
("The Journal of Sexual Medicine"[Journal]) AND
(
  "sexual dysfunction" OR "erectile dysfunction" OR "premature ejaculation" OR
  "female sexual dysfunction" OR "hypoactive sexual desire disorder" OR
  dyspareunia OR orgasm* OR libido OR arousal
)
```

### 2. Sexual Medicine Reviews
- **Positioning:** Evidence-based review journal in sexual medicine; high-yield for overviews, mechanisms, and treatment summaries.
- **Best Quartile:** **Q1**
- **Common keywords:** systematic review, scoping review, sexual function, sexual health, sexual dysfunction, psychosexual intervention, hormones, pharmacotherapy, surgery, transgender sexual health
- **PubMed template:**
```text
("Sexual Medicine Reviews"[Journal]) AND
(
  review OR "systematic review" OR meta-analysis OR "scoping review"
) AND
(
  "sexual function" OR "sexual health" OR "sexual dysfunction" OR psychosexual OR libido
)
```

## 6) Suggested Starting Strategy

- Use **The Journal of Sexual Medicine** and **Sexual Medicine Reviews** first for **clinical treatment questions**.
- Use **Archives of Sexual Behavior** and **The Journal of Sex Research** first for **broad sexology / psychology questions**.
- Use **Journal of Sex & Marital Therapy** for **psychosexual therapy and couple-based interventions**.
- Use **Psychoneuroendocrinology** and **Hormones and Behavior** for **sexual neuroscience / hormone-brain-behavior mechanisms**.
- Use **International Journal of Sexual Health**, **Sexual Health**, and **Sexuality Research and Social Policy** for **sexual health systems, public health, rights, and implementation**.
